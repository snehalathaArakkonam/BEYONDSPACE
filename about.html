OUR STORY

BEYONDSPACE (SPACERIDE2026) was born in 2025 in Andhra Pradesh when a group of school kids and college students dreamed of making space tourism affordable for every Indian child. Inspired by ISRO’s historic achievements, we created this futuristic platform to educate, entertain and launch the next generation of Indian astronauts.

Mission: Make every Indian kid say “I want to go to space!” by 2030
